FotoView.NET Release!

24.06.2020, 14:32

If you want a light foto viewer that doesn't change all of your icons to pictures of cats, then you're in the right place! FotoView.NET allows you to open your photos quickly, it's simple to use and works on Windows versions newer than Windows XP. It is also a standalone program so no need to install it!
